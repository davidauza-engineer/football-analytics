Here's another cool trick Python has up its sleeve to print several lines at once: using three single (''') or three double (""") quotes. Check it out:

print('''My contact:
Email: your_name@gmail.com
Phone: +xx-1234-5678''')

This neat little snippet will give you:

My contact:
Email: your_name@gmail.com
Phone: +xx-1234-5678

Pretty handy, right?
