Let's move forward to the next set of operations. We know you may have guessed the symbols for multiplication and division. To be thorough, here are examples to illustrate multiplication and division:

a = 10 
b = 2 
product = a * b 
division = a / b
print(product)
print(division)

In this context, the \(*\) symbol signifies multiplication, and \(/\) denotes division.
