Let's spice things up a bit! You can blend together the arithmetic operations we've covered to craft more complex expressions. Keep in mind the order of precedence for these operators. Here's the hierarchy:

Parentheses > Exponentiation > Multiplication/Division/Integer Division/Modulo > Addition/Subtraction

What's with the parentheses () reigning supreme at the top? Good observation! While not an operation itself, parentheses serve to clarify the order of execution. Anything within parentheses gets calculated before any operations outside. They're like the directors calling the shots on which operations to execute first. Pretty dominating, huh?
